Q1 ordered DELPHI EXPERT CONSULTATION - ROUND 1 We are grateful for your participation in developing evidence-informed practical guidance for frontline staff engaging in transcultural conversations with asylum seekers about mental health and the potential need for Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (MHPSS). As a member of our expert panel, your insights are highly valued. In this first round, you will encounter a brief series of enrollment questions, followed by two open-ended questions focused on practical guidelines and professional competences. We aim to gather a diverse array of perspectives on the essential guidelines and competences for frontline workers aiding asylum seekers in collective reception centres. Upon collecting all responses, we will compile them into a series of anonymized statements for you to review. In subsequent rounds, you will rate your agreement with each statement. Please be assured that your contributions and evaluations of others’ statements will remain confidential. For further details on the ethical considerations of this study, click here.
